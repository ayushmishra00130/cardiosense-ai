    Heart Care Guide

    Simple Actions to Protect Your Heart

    Use these easy steps to stay safe, especially if you feel warning symptoms.

    Now

    What to do during a possible heart attack

    • Call emergency services right away.
    • Sit down, stay calm, and loosen tight clothes.
    • Do not drive yourself to the hospital.
    Today

    What to do to lower risk now

    • Check your blood pressure and record it.
    • Choose low-salt and low-oil meals.
    • Stop smoking and avoid alcohol binges.
    Every Week

    Habits to keep your heart strong

    • Walk or exercise for at least 150 minutes per week.
    • Sleep 7 to 8 hours daily.
    • Plan one stress-relief activity you enjoy.

    Do / Don't Quick Table

    Do Don't
    Eat fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. Eat too much fried and processed food.
    Take medicines as advised by your doctor. Skip blood pressure or diabetes medicines.
    Get regular health checkups. Ignore chest pain or breathing problems.
    Stay active most days of the week. Sit for long hours without movement.

    If you are having a medical emergency, contact emergency services immediately.
